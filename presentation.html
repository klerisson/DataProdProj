This R Markdown presentation about the interactive NOOA data set. Goal of this report is to idenify severe weather events of the highest impact on both US population and US economy based on the information retrieved from U.S. National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's (NOAA) storm database.

Results

Most of the observed differences can be attributed to the differences in the size of the samples. Nevertheless I was able to identift four types of events which had both high normalized average and maximum impact and should be considered highly harmful:

  • Surge
  • Heat / Heat Wave
  • Tropical storm
  • Tsunami
